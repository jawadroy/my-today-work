Rain Harvesting Techniques

Rain Barrels

Rain barrels collect and store rainwater from rooftops through downspouts. They are an affordable and efficient way to collect water for garden use.

Rain Gardens

Rain gardens are designed to capture and absorb rainwater runoff from impervious surfaces like driveways and rooftops. They help reduce stormwater runoff and improve water quality.
