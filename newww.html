Technique 1: Simple Collection

This technique involves collecting rainwater from roofs or other surfaces into a storage container. It's straightforward and cost-effective.

Technique 2: Infiltration Systems

Infiltration systems allow rainwater to percolate through the ground, replenishing groundwater supplies. This method is beneficial for sustainable water management.

Technique 3: Rain Gardens

Rain gardens are designed to capture and absorb rainwater runoff from impervious surfaces, helping to reduce stormwater pollution and erosion.
